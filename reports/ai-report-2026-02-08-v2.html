Category: Open Source

Background: As LLM-based agents transition from simple chatbots to autonomous entities capable of executing complex workflows, the primary bottleneck has shifted from intelligence to interoperability. Historically, agents operated in silos or required human-in-the-loop interfaces; A2A protocols aim to solve this by providing a standardized 'language' for agents to hire each other, share context, and settle payments. This trend connects to the broader 'Agentic Web' movement, where Web3 infrastructure provides the trustless layer needed for AI agents to hold capital and enter into programmatic contracts.
